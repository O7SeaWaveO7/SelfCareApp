# SelfCare AI Mood & Habit Tracking App

## Overview

SelfCare AI is a mobile self-care app that helps users improve their mental wellbeing, habits, and daily routines through mood tracking, habit tracking, and AI-powered insights.

The app goes beyond simple tracking by analyzing patterns between moods and habits, then providing personalized suggestions to help users feel better, stay consistent, and build healthier routines.

---

## Core Idea

Most self-care apps only let users _log data_.

SelfCare AI helps users **understand their data and improve their life**.

It connects:

- Mood changes
- Daily habits
- Sleep, exercise, and routine behavior

And turns them into **actionable AI insights**.

---

## Key Features

### Mood Tracking

- Daily mood check-ins
- Simple emotion selection (happy, stressed, tired, calm, anxious, etc.)
- Mood history and trends over time

### Habit Tracking

- Custom habit creation (e.g. sleep, water, exercise, journaling)
- Streak tracking
- Completion reminders

### AI Insights (Core Feature)

- Detects patterns between habits and mood
- Generates personalized suggestions like:
  - “You feel better on days you exercise”
  - “Your stress increases when you sleep late”
  - “You are most productive in the morning”

### Smart Notifications

- Mood check-in reminders
- Habit reminders
- Personalized nudges based on behavior patterns

Example:

- “How are you feeling today?”
- “You haven’t checked in your mood yet”
- “Try a short walk — it usually improves your mood”

---

## AI System Description

The app collects user data over time:

- Mood entries
- Habit completion history
- Time of day patterns

This data is sent to an AI model that:

1. Identifies behavioral patterns
2. Finds correlations between habits and mood
3. Generates simple, supportive recommendations
4. Adapts suggestions over time as more data is collected

The AI acts like a **personal wellbeing coach**, not just a tracker.
